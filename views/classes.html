Classes

Wrestling

Our practices are designed to build both skill and resilience. We start with conditioning to warm up, improve mobility, and to help prevent injuries. We follow up by focusing on techniques and drilling to refine the skills necessary for success. Each session ends with live wrestling, where we can apply what we,ve learned in real-time scenarios. Whether you're new to the sport or a seasoned wrestler, our hardworking and supportive environment is perfect for developing your abilities and reaching your full potential.

Open Mat

Our open mat practice sessions provide a relaxed and flexible environment for wrestlers to drop in at their convenience. During open mat, there is no structured practice; instead, students are encouraged to explore new techniques, ask questions, and wrestle with one another. This informal setting fosters creativity, learning, and camaraderie, allowing you to refine your skills and connect with teammates. Whether you're looking to try out new moves or simply enjoy some friendly sparring, our open mat sessions are a great way to grow and socialize within our wrestling community.

Jiu Jitsu

Our Jiu Jitsu classes are structured to provide a comprehensive and engaging training experience. We begin with a warm-up to prepare your body, followed by in-depth instruction on techniques and concepts to enhance your grappling skills. Each session concludes with live rolling, allowing you to put what you've learned into practice. While we primarily focus on no-gi Jiu Jitsu, we also offer gi practice upon request. Join us to improve your technique, build strength, and become part of our supportive and dedicated Jiu Jitsu community.

Workout Sessions

Our workout sessions are all about staying active and breaking a sweat. These intense, high-energy workouts focus on cardio and conditioning, keeping you constantly moving to improve endurance and mobility. Through a variety of aerobic exercises, we help you build stamina and strength while having fun. These sessions are perfect for anyone looking to boost their fitness levels, enhance their athletic performance, or lose weight in a supportive and motivating environment. Join us for a challenging and rewarding workout that will leave you feeling accomplished and energized.
