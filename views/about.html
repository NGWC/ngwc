About NG Wrestling Club

At New Gen Wrestling Club, we welcome anyone who is curious about the greatest and oldest sport, wrestling, and hope to inspire those to join us in building better versions of ourselves. Students of all skill levels and backgrounds are encouraged to come and train. While this sport is an amazing opportunity to strengthen our bodies, it also allows us to connect and learn from one another. NGWC aims to foster an encouraging and friendly environment where teamates build each other up. Feel free to stop by to speak with the coaches and consider signing up for a free trial! See you on the mat!

Head Coach Harvey Robbins

Harvey Robbins is the current head coach of NGWC and is passionate about learning and teaching wrestling. Robbins started his wrestling career while he was a young boy and competed in the State Championship Tournament as a highschooler in his home state of Georgia. He continued to train and compete at the amatuer level, winning and placing in several local tournaments. Robbins is also a brown belt in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu and has medaled in several local tournaments.

Coach David Rock

Coach David is the current Jiu Jitsu coach and assists head coach Robbins during wrestling practice. David has an extensive background in athletic training with a degree in Sport Physiology. He is passionate about training and staying healthy and active. David is a Jiu Jitsu brown belt and competes at the local tournaments. He is focused on sharing the benefits of wrestling and grappling to new students as well as refining the skills of seasoned competitors.
