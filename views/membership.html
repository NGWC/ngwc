Membership

Single

Single adult membership contracts are $100/month. No initiation fee.

Family

Family membership contracts start at $75/month for the first member and $50/month per additional member. No initiation fees.

Scholarships / Discounts

NGWC is open to anyone who is interested in wrestling and learning, and we believe that a price should not limit ones desire to learn. We offer discounts for first responders and additional scholarships for those who need assistance with the fees. All discounts and scholarships must be approved by the gym owner.
